Anthizo Festival Info

Anthizo 2022 will be held on March 19th and March 20th, 2022! Anthizo's shows will carry on rain or shine! Check out What to Bring section to be prepared!

What to Bring

Be prepared to have the best time at Anthizo! Below are acceptable items to bring to the event to keep you and everyone safe.

  • ✓ Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Masks
  • ✓ Ear plugs
  • ✓ Binoculars
  • ✓ Blankets and beach towels
  • ✓ Sun block (non‐aerosol)
  • ✓ Small bags (20 x 20 inches or smaller)
  • ✓ Warm pullover or hoodie (for the night)
  • ✓ Rain jacket (for the rain!)
  • ✓ Vapes and E-cigarettes
  • ✓ Water bottles (MUST be EMPTY) (Plastic, reusable) (camelbaks of 64oz. or less)
  • ✓ Inhalers
  • ✓ Gum
  • ✓ Make‐up
  • ✓ Hand sanitizer
  • ✓ Lighters
  • ✓ Cameras (digital/film with NO detachable lens, disposable, polaroid)
  • ✓ GoPro's (NO poles or extenders)
  • ✓ Rechargeable Batteries (Mophie)

What's NOT Allowed

Items marked by security as inappropriate or dangerous are not allowed. Violating these rules will lead to confiscation of Items and guests may be asked to leave if displaying dangerous behavior with the item.

  • ✗ Aerosol Products
  • ✗ Air Horns
  • ✗ Audio Recording Devices
  • ✗ Balloons, Balls, Inflatable Balls, Frisbees
  • ✗ Bicycle, Skates, Skateboards, Scooters, Hoverboards
  • ✗ Body Armor
  • ✗ Chains, Tape, Rope
  • ✗ Chairs, Tents, Umbrellas
  • ✗ Laser pointers
  • ✗ LED or glow lights
  • ✗ Metal/Steel/Aluminum Water Bottles
  • ✗ Pets
  • ✗ Squirt Guns
  • ✗ Spray Paint or Markers
  • ✗ Selfie sticks
  • ✗ Vape or E‐cigarette Liquid Refills
  • ✗ Weapons of any kind
  • ✗ Walkie‐talkies

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Health & Safety

How do I show proof of vaccination?

Acceptable forms of vaccination include the FRONT and BACK of the following:

  • ▸ A physical or digital copy of a COVID‐19 Vaccination Record Card issued by CDC
  • ▸ Valid government‐issued ID to identify you

What happens if I feel sick before the event?

Please stay home and rest. Follow the mandated protocols for your symptoms and take a rapid COVID-19 test when necessary. We want to ensure everyone’s safety for a good experience. We’ll see you next year if you’re feeling unwell!

What happens if I start to feel sick during the event?

Please visit the medical tent at the venue to receive a rapid COVID-19 test.

If the guest tests positive, their wristband will be cut/deactivated and must leave the festival. Guests testing positive are encouraged to notify anyone they were in close contact with about the positive COVID-19 test result.

Ordering Tickets & Box Office

What are the ticket tiers?

Tickets can be bought for 1 Day, 2 Days, and VIP 2 Days with additional perks. You can choose from three ticket tiers:

  1. ▸ Tier 1: Early Bird Admission
  2. ▸ Tier 2: General Admission
  3. ▸ Tier 3: VIP (19+, ID Required)

VIP ticket holders get access to VIP‐only bathrooms, VIP bar, discounted merch, VIP-exclusive lanyard and wristband, food and drink vouchers, and entry to the After party.

How many tickets can I buy at once?

Each buyer can purchase a maximum of four (4) tickets per day per order. Please note prices do not include service fees until checkout page.

Can I refund my tickets?

Unless otherwise stated, all sales are final. No refunds, exchanges, or cancellations.

I bought my tickets from someone else. Can I attend the event?

Tickets from unauthorized third parties are NOT valid. Only tickets purchased from our official site are valid for the Anthizo Festival.

Lockers

Will there be lockers or storage spaces?

Yes! Lockers are available for rent. Reserve a locker ahead of time to save space for your belongings. You will have unlimited access to your locker for the duration of your rental period.

Subscribe to our newsletter for updates on when you can start reserving lockers online.

How long can I rent lockers for?

Lockers can be reserved and rented for half a day or a full day. You can also reserve lockers for both days.

Please check the daily and weekend rates when you can start reserving lockers online.

Accessibility

Are service animals permitted?

Yes, service animals that are NOT emotional support animals are permitted in the venue area. Service animals must stay at handler's side and use the designated service animal relief area.

Handlers are responsible for any damage or injury to their service animal. Service animals should have their required vaccinations.

How do I access the event in my wheelchair?

The venue has cement terrain with a turf overlay. At this time, we do not have any wheelchair or scooters for rental.

Please visit the Accessibility Services kiosk for an accessible viewing area during the event.

Lost & Found

What if I forget something at the event?

Please label your belongings in case you forget your items at the event. We will try our best to locate all lost items to contact the owner after the event.

Lost items are kept for 30 calendar days after the event. If the owner does not claim the items, lost items will be discarded.
